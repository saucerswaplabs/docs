You can connect a wallet to the SaucerSwap web app using a desktop browser.

1. Visit SaucerSwap web app.
2. Click "Connect Wallet".
3. Choose your Hedera-supported wallet (e.g., HashPack, Kabila).
4. Authorize the connection from your wallet interface.
