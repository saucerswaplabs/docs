---
title: "How to change slippage on the SaucerSwap web app"
description: "Description of your new file."
---

1. Go to the **Swap** page on SaucerSwap.
2. Click the **Settings** icon (gear) near the top of the swap interface.
3. Adjust the **Slippage Tolerance** by choosing a preset (like 0.1%, 0.5%, 1%) or entering a custom number.
4. Close settings, and your new slippage will apply to your next swap.
