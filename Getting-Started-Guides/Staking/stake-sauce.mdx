To stake your SAUCE:

1. Connect your wallet to SaucerSwap.
2. Navigate to the **Staking** or **Infinity Pool** section.
3. Click **Stake** next to SAUCE or the relevant pool.

   [PG-029]
4. Enter the amount of SAUCE you want to stake.
5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

   [PG-030]
