Recommended Hedera-compatible wallets: **HashPack, Blade, Kabila, MetaMask**.

Download and set up these wallets from their official websites or app stores.

HashPack Wallet: [<u>https://www.hashpack.app/</u>](https://www.hashpack.app/)

Blade Wallet: [<u>https://bladewallet.io/</u>](https://bladewallet.io/)

Kabila Wallet: [<u>https://www.kabila.app/wallet</u>](https://www.kabila.app/wallet)

MetaMask: [<u>https://metamask.io/</u>](https://metamask.io/)
