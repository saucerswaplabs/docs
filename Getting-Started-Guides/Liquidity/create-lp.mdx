title: "How do I create a liquidity pool (V1)?"
description: "Description of your new file."
---

To create a new liquidity pool on SaucerSwap V1:

1. Connect your wallet.
2. Go to the **Pools** section and click **Create Pool**.

   [PG-017]
3. Select the two tokens you want to pair.
4. Set the **initial price**.
5. Deposit the correct amount of each token.

   [PG-018]
6. Confirm the creation and pay the network and pool creation fees (\$50) in HBAR.

Once created, anyone (including you) can add liquidity to the pool\!
