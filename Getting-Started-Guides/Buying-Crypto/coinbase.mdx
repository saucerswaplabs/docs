---
title: "How to transfer crypto from a Coinbase account"
description: "Description of your new file."
---

1. Log in to your Coinbase account.
2. Go to the **Assets** tab and select the crypto you want to send.
3. Click **Send**, paste your **destination wallet address**, and enter the amount.

   [PG-005]
4. Confirm the transaction and pay any network fees.
5. The crypto will arrive in the receiving wallet, often within a few minutes.

   [PG-006]

Make sure you select the correct network (e.g., Hedera for HBAR)\!
