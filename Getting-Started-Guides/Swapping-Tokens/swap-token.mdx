description: "Description of your new file."

To swap tokens on SaucerSwap:

1. Connect your wallet to SaucerSwap.
2. Choose the token you want to swap **from** and the token you want to swap **to**.
3. Enter the amount.

   [PG-032]
4. Review the slippage tolerance and swap details.
5. Confirm the swap and approve the transaction in your wallet.

   [PG-033]

Your new tokens will appear in your wallet once the swap is completed\!
