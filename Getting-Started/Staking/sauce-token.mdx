SAUCE (0.0.731861) is the governance and rewards token for SaucerSwap.\
It’s used to:

- Vote on protocol decisions.
- Earn staking rewards.
- Capture a portion of the platform’s revenue (through mechanisms like xSAUCE).

Holding and staking SAUCE gives you a bigger voice and more rewards in the SaucerSwap ecosystem.
