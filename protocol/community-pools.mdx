<Note>
  Community Pools have not launched. As of July 2026 the feature is not available in the [web app](https://www.saucerswap.finance/); this page describes the proposed design. See the [roadmap](/roadmap) for current status.
</Note>

Community Pools are a proposed launch mechanism for new projects entering the SaucerSwap ecosystem: projects distribute a portion of their token supply over time to users who stake xSAUCE, giving projects fast access to a committed community without raising venture capital or listing on a centralized exchange, and giving xSAUCE holders a way to earn new tokens without impermanent loss risk.

## Proposed design

- **Vetting.** Eligible projects would complete an accelerator or vetting process covering code security, ethical practices, and development integrity before becoming candidates.
- **Governance approval.** The [SaucerSwap DAO](/governance/overview) would vote to grant a Community Pool to a project, and could separately vote to allocate farm or LARI emissions to the project's liquidity pools.
- **Pool creation.** The project would allocate a percentage of its token's max supply to be distributed to stakers over a defined period.
- **xSAUCE staking.** Users would stake xSAUCE in the Community Pool and earn the project's token, without exposure to impermanent loss.

Details of the design may change before launch, and launch itself is subject to governance and product decisions.
